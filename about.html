About Us

Our company, which has 60 years of experience in the fields of leather goods and textile, has added cold press seed oil machinery into our product range by combining our strength and experiences with our new partner who has great experience in cold press seed oil machinery.

Our Company produces cold press seed oil machines in our modern production facilities with high quality materials without compromising our principles by using stainless steel.

Our products are produced as a result of studies of our R&D team with great care and innovation. Comparing with other products in the market, our products are more functional, and practical. We also are able to produce custom designs according to your requests.

Our Sales and Marketing team works fast, effective and in a great discipline in order to answer your needs and requests of spare parts and units.
